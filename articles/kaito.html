Kaito Kid

Written on 7 November 2020

Kaito Kid is an anime about a guy named Kaito Kuroba.

He wears white clothing and his most notable accessories are a cylinder and a monocle.

The anime somewhat follows the monster of the day principle, but with crimes. In each episode Kaito steals something in a very sneaky way using tricks and "magic".

It's quite a funny anime and I can recommend it especially for young people.

If you are older, you might have already heard of Kaito, as he's a character from the Detective Conan series.
